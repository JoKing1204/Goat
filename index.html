About

Mike Tyson is an American former professional boxer. He is considered to be one of the greatest heavyweight boxers of all time. Tyson was known for his ferocious and intimidating boxing style2and was ranked 16th on The Ring magazine's list of 100 greatest punchers of all time. He was also known as "The Baddest Man on the Planet". Tyson has gone on to appear in several films and a Broadway show on his life, become a best-selling author and an entrepreneur.

1981 He won gold medals at the 1981 and 1982 Junior Olympic Games, defeating Joe Cortez and Kelton Brown.
1986 He was the Youngest Heavyweight Champion by age 20 in 1986.
1987 He won the WBA, WBC, and IBF heavyweight titles and unified them in 1987 by defeating James Smith and Tony Tucker.
